Our Privacy policy

Penguin Publishing and our privacy policy

Penguin Publishing is a local school news site that's main purpose is to deliver quality articles to Students, teachers and parents. Here is our Privacy policy regarding the basic and personal information we may ask you for.


If you're using our Website, we automatically collect some basic information and ask you for some personal information that most websites collect, where necessary and to your consent.
This is done by asking you directly either Via Email or directly through the website itself.This includes information about how you use the website, such as the pages you view, how you got to the site and the date and time you accessed the website, your name, contact information, interactions with us and Cookies.

We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our Websites. All data is stored onsite in a secure set of servers and backed up offline. You can access your own information at anytime by emailing us with proof it's you and we will send it back to you ASAP. Our Websites use cookies, tagging and other tracking technologies to help us to enhance or personalize your online experience.

We use your information to help us personalize and continually improve your experience on the Websites, including fulfilling your requests for information, analyzing trends and statistics, and communicating with you. At any time you can change your information just by contacting one of our staff members. For information about how to manage your information and the choices you have, please email one of us here.
